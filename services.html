Welcome to Ethan Logistics Website

Our
Services

We specialise in the transportation of a variety of loose and packed goods throughout the Southern Africa region. We are moved by our core values of transparency, integrity, customer satisfaction and professionalism to successfully move your business through the challenges facing the transport industry of today.

1. National Transportation

We specialize in reliable national transportation, offering seamless movement of goods across the country. Our modern fleet ensures safe and timely delivery for businesses of all sizes, connecting suppliers to customers efficiently. We prioritize the logistics needs of various industries, ensuring that goods are delivered swiftly, from city centers to rural areas, with top-notch service.

2. Cross Border Transport

We provide cross-border transport services, expertly handling the complexities of international trade. We ensure that goods move across borders smoothly, with a strong focus on compliance, customs documentation, and security. Our cross-border solutions guarantee timely deliveries to neighboring countries, supporting businesses in expanding their reach and simplifying global trade processes.

3. Short Distance Haulage

We also offer short-distance haulage services offer swift, efficient transport for goods within local or regional areas. Perfect for businesses needing quick deliveries, we ensure reliable transport for last-mile logistics, construction materials, and daily operations. Our fleet is equipped to handle short routes with speed and precision, meeting your local business needs.

4. Long Distance Haulage

We provide dependable long-distance haulage services, ideal for businesses requiring large-scale transport across extended routes. We specialize in moving goods safely and efficiently between cities or states, ensuring timely deliveries of bulk shipments. Our long-distance haulage is perfect for manufacturers, distributors, and retailers, guaranteeing secure and cost-effective logistics.

5. Taut Liners

We offer taut liner services, utilizing curtain-sided trucks for flexible and efficient transport. Our taut liners are designed for easy loading and unloading, making them ideal for transporting diverse cargo types such as pallets, crates, and oversized goods. This solution ensures quick operations while maintaining the safety and integrity of your goods during transit.
